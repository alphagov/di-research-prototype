Driving licence authoritative source check

Phase
READY FOR BUILD
Designers
Leigh Christie, Mark Woods
Summary

We will be introducing authoritative source checks (via DVLA / DVA) for users who have successful used a UK driving licence in the app. We need to consider the user experience of this and design a few specific elements:

  • capture of consent
  • replaying the details captured in the app journey
  • a way for the user to confirm whether these details are accurate
  • a sensible escape route(s) if the details are not accurate
