Prove your identity with GOV.UK One Login

You’ll need:

  • a GOV.UK One Login - we’ll ask you to sign in or create one when you continue
  • your photo ID
  • your current home address (you might also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved)
If you do not have photo ID

You may be able to prove your identity using a current account with a UK bank or building society.

You’ll also need to:

  • provide your National Insurance number
  • answer some security questions

We’ll only use the information you give us to make sure that you are who you say you are.

You can also use GOV.UK One Login in Welsh (Cymraeg).
