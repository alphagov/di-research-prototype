Scan the QR code to prove your identity by signing in to your online banking

  1. Open the camera app on your phone or tablet.
  2. Position your camera over the QR code. Wait until your camera recognises the QR code and a link appears on your screen.
  3. Tap the link on your screen to open your bank’s app or website so you can continue proving your identity.

    This page will automatically update once you scan the QR code. You can refresh the page if it doesn’t update.

If you have a problem using the QR code, you can stay on this device and use your bank’s website.

For help, contact the GOV.UK One Login team (opens in a new tab).
