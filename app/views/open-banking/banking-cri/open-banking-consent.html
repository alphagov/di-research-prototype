Agree to share information from your bank account

To prove your identity using open banking, you need to share some information with Ecospend.

This is to check your account is real and protect against fraud.

Information that’s shared

Ecospend will have one-off access to check:

  • your account number and sort code
  • that your account is active (by confirming there are recent transactions - they will not share details of the transactions)
Your information will be securely deleted after the check is complete.

Information that’s not shared

Ecospend will not:

  • see your online banking sign-in details
  • use your information to take payments

None of your bank account information will be shared with the government service you’re using.

Give approval for your bank account information to be shared

Our privacy notice (opens in new tab) has more detail about how your information will be used.
