Agree to share information from your bank or building society account

We use 2 companies to help us check your account information and confirm your identity:

  • Crosina Capital
  • Judentia

Both are regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (opens in a new tab).

Information that’s shared with Crosina Capital and Judentia

Your bank will share your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • account number
  • sort code
  • recent transactions, to confirm that your account has been active in the past 3 months (we will not see details of the transactions)

Information that’s not shared

Crosina Capital and Judentia will not:

  • have access to your online banking sign-in details
  • use your information to take payments

None of your bank account information will be shared with the government service you’re using.
