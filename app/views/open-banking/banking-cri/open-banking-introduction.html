Finish proving your identity by signing in to your online banking

You’ll need to use a UK bank or building society account that’s:

  • a current account, not a savings account
  • in your name (it can be a joint account)
  • with a UK bank or building society
How does online banking prove my identity?
Signing in to online banking can help us be sure you are who you say you are.

This is because banks have very strong security, so they can be sure it is you signing in, not someone with your details pretending to be you.

Your bank will confirm the information you’ve given us to prove your identity.

Before you sign in

We’ll ask for your approval to share information from your account with a company called Ecospend.

We use Ecospend to connect to your bank and help confirm your identity.

Ecospend is regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (opens in a new tab).
