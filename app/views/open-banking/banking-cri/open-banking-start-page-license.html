Prove your identity by signing in to your online banking

1

Enter details from your UK photocard driving licence


You can use a UK driving licence if it:

  • has your photo
  • has not expired

It can be a full or provisional UK driving licence.

2

Enter your address


You might also need to enter your previous address if you’ve recently moved.

2

Sign in to online banking


You’ll need to sign in to your online banking and agree to share some information so we can confirm your identity.

Who you share your information with
We use 2 companies to hel23p us check your bank account information and confirm your identity: Crosina Capital and Judentia. Both companies are regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (opens in a new tab).

Your bank will share your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • account number
  • sort code
  • recent transactions, to confirm your account has been active in the past 3 months (we will not see details of the transactions)

Your bank will not share your online banking sign in details.

3

Continue to the service you were trying to use


After you have proved your identity, you can continue to the service you were trying to use.

I want to prove my identity another way

Steps to proving your identity

  • 1. Get your details checked

    This includes your passport, driving licence, or other official ID.

  • 2. Enter your address

    Make sure both are clear and well-lit for verification purposes.

  • 3. Sign into your online banking

    You'll get a confirmation once your identity is verified.
