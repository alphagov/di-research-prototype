Prove your identity by signing in to your online banking

1

Enter details from your photo ID


You can enter details from a:

  • UK photocard driving licence (full or provisional)
  • UK passport
2

Enter your address


You might also need to enter your previous address if you’ve recently moved.

2

Sign in to online banking


You’ll need to sign in to your online banking and agree to share some information so we can confirm your identity.

Who you share your information with
We use 2 companies to help us check your bank account information and confirm your identity: Crosina Capital and Judentia. Both companies are regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (opens in a new tab).

Your bank will share your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • account number
  • sort code
  • recent transactions, to confirm your account has been active in the past 3 months (we will not see details of the transactions)

Your bank will not share your online banking sign in details.

3

Continue to the service you were trying to use


After you have proved your identity, you can continue to the service you were trying to use.
