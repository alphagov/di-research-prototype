If your driving licence details are not correct

You can scan your UK photocard driving licence in the GOV.UK ID Check app again.

Make sure your driving licence does not have any dirt or scratches on the front.

When you scan your driving licence, make sure you’re in a well-lit area with no glare or reflection on your licence.
