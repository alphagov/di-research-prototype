Continue proving your identity online

Based on what you told us, you cannot prove your identity using the app.

You may be able to prove your identity by signing in to your online banking and sharing some information from your account instead. This is called ‘open banking’.

You’ll need:

  • a UK bank or building society account that’s been open at least 3 months
  • a UK photocard driving licence or a UK passport
    • {% if (data['confidence'] == "low") %}
  • National Insurance number
    • {% endif %}
How does online banking prove my identity?
Signing in to online banking can help us be sure you are who you say you are.

This is because banks have very strong security, so they can be sure it is you signing in, not someone with your details pretending to be you.

Your bank will confirm the information you’ve given us to prove your identity.
