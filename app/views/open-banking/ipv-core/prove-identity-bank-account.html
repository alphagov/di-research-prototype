Prove your identity with bank or building society details and security questions

You can use any current account with a UK bank or building society to prove your identity. Your name must be on the account.

Provide some personal details

We’ll ask you for your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • sort code and account number
  • National Insurance number
  • current address (you might also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved)

Answer some security questions

We’ll then ask you some security questions. This helps us check if the details you've given are being used by you.
