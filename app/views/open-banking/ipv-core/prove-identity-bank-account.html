Prove your identity by signing in to your online banking

You can continue proving your identity online by providing some personal details.

You’ll also need to share information from your bank or building society account.

We do this to stop anyone who might have your details from pretending to be you.

Provide some personal details

We’ll ask you for your:

  • name as it appears on your bank or building society account
  • National Insurance number
  • current address (you might also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved)

Share information from your bank account

When we ask you to, you’ll need to sign in to your online banking to share some information. This is called ‘open banking’.
