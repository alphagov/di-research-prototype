Answer questions about your financial history

We’ll ask you some questions about your financial history that only you should know the answers to.

This helps us to stop someone who might have your details pretending to be you.

The questions will be about things like your mobile phone contract, bank account, and any credit cards, loans or mortgages you have.

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "Do not guess the answer if you do not know it. You cannot change your answers and you cannot skip any questions.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

We will not keep your answers or share them with any other organisation.

How we know what questions to ask you

We’ll work with Experian (opens in new tab) to make sure we ask you questions that only you can answer. This is because companies like Experian have access to lots of information that we can check your answers against. We’ll only ask you questions about information that Experian already has.

Our privacy notice (opens in new tab) explains how we use your details to prove your identity.
