You can change how you get security codes to sign in to your GOV.UK One Login

You will need to confirm this is your GOV.UK One Login first.

What you need to do

  • Prove your identity with the GOV.UK ID Check app.
  • Enter your current home address (you may also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved).
  • We’ll check your identity matches the one on your GOV.UK One Login.
  • Set up a new way to get security codes - this can be a phone number or an authenticator app.
  • Sign in to GOV.UK One Login and continue to the service you are trying to use.
