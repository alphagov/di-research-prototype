Continue proving your identity online

You can continue proving your identity online if you:

  • have a UK driving licence (full or provisional) or a UK passport
  • can answer some security questions

We use security questions to stop anyone who might have your details from pretending to be you.

It takes about 10 minutes to prove your identity this way.
