Prove your identity with your National Insurance number and security questions

As well as your National Insurance number, we also need your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • current address (you might also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved)

We’ll then ask you some security questions. We use these to stop anyone who might have your details from pretending to be you.
