Update your name using the GOV.UK ID Check app

To update your name, you need to prove your identity again with your new name.

You'll need:

  • photo ID with the name you want GOV.UK One Login to use
  • to tell us where you’ve lived for the last 3 months

Find out what photo ID you can use to prove your identity (opens in new tab).

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "You may not be able to use your GOV.UK One Login if we cannot confirm your identity using your new details.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

If there is a mistake in your name

If there is a mistake in your name, do not try to prove your identity again unless the GOV.UK One Login support team has told you to.

For example, if your name is missing letters or a hyphen, contact the GOV.UK One Login team (opens in new tab) instead.

If you need to use the service now

Your proof of identity is still valid even if your details are out of date.

You can use your saved details to access the service you need to use.
