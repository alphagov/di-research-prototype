Go to a Post Office within 15 days to finish proving your identity

You’ve been sent a confirmation email with a link to download your Post Office customer letter. If you asked us to, we'll also send your customer letter to you by post.

To finish proving your identity, you need to go to a Post Office that offers in-branch verification within the next 15 days.

{{ govukInsetText({ text: "If you do not go to a Post Office within the next 15 days, you’ll need to start proving your identity again." }) }}

What you need to take to a Post Office

When you go to a Post Office, you must take:

  • your Post Office customer letter
  • the photo ID shown on your Post Office customer letter

Which Post Office to go to

Your customer letter shows which Post Office you chose to go to.

You can go to a different Post Office as long as it offers in-branch verification (opens in new tab).

You do not need to contact the GOV.UK One Login team if you decide to go to a different Post Office.

At the Post Office

You’ll need to show your Post Office customer letter on your phone or tablet, or take a printed copy with you.

A member of Post Office staff will:

  • check your customer letter
  • scan your photo ID
  • take a photo of you

After you've been to the Post Office

You’ll get an email about the result of your identity check - usually within a day of going to the Post Office.

If you need help

Contact the GOV.UK One Login support team (opens in new tab)
