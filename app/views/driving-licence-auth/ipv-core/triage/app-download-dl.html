Scan the QR code to download or open the GOV.UK ID Check app

Use the camera on your phone to scan the QR code below. We’ll give you step by step instructions on how to use the app when you open it.

How to scan a QR code
  1. Open the camera app on your phone.
  2. Position your camera so the QR code is visible on your screen.
  3. Wait until the QR code is recognised by your camera and a link appears on your screen.
  4. Tap the link to go to the App Store.
  5. Download the GOV.UK ID Check app.

Downloading the app without using the QR code

If you cannot use the QR code, you can still download the app by opening the App Store on your phone and searching for the ‘GOV.UK ID Check’ app

I’d prefer not to use the app

If you’d prefer not to use the app, you may still be able to confirm your identity another way.

You’ll still need photo ID and other ways of confirming your identity may not provide an instant result.

Problems using the app? You can confirm your identity another way.

For additional help, contact the GOV.UK ID Check support team.
