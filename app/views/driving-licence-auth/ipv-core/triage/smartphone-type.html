Tell us what smartphone you are using now

To prove your identity with the GOV.UK One Login app, you need an iPhone or Android phone with a working camera.

If your photo ID is a UK photocard driving licence

You need an iPhone 6s or newer, or an Android phone.

If your photo ID is a passport, BRP, BRC or FWP

You need an iPhone 7 or newer, or an Android phone.

How do I know which iPhone I have?

The iPhone 7 was released in 2016. If you bought your iPhone after 2016, you probably have an iPhone 7 or newer.

To check which iPhone you have, go to your phone settings. Select ‘General’ then ‘About’ and look for the ‘Model Name’.
